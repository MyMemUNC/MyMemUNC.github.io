About Us

The UNC Foundation

The UNC Foundation's primary goal is to secure and contain these anomalous objects in order to protect humanity from their potentially dangerous effects.
The Foundation operates on a global scale, with branches and facilities located all over the world.
Its members, known as "Foundation personnel," are highly trained and specialized in a variety of fields, including science, engineering, and containment procedures.
We work tirelessly to monitor and contain anomalous phenomena, often at great personal risk.
The Foundation is shrouded in secrecy, with the public at large completely unaware of its existence.
It operates outside of government oversight and is funded through a variety of covert means.
