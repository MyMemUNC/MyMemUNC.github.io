Experimentation Guidelines

Following Incident [A-L-024b], and in light of the extreme hazardous properties and anomalous effects of A-L-024, all direct experimentation involving A-L-024 is strictly prohibited. Research activities are restricted exclusively to indirect data analysis and must utilize non-optical sensors or approved non-visual observation techniques. Any experimental proposal involving A-L-024 requires authorization from no fewer than two Level A personnel and must be accompanied by a comprehensive risk assessment report. Unauthorized experimental activity will result in immediate quarantine and disciplinary action.


All data records related to A-L-024—including, but not limited to, sensor outputs, environmental monitoring logs, and indirect observational results—must be encrypted and stored on isolated servers, accessible solely to Level A personnel. Any attempt to observe A-L-024 through visual means—including unaided eye contact, real-time video transmission, or optical instrumentation—constitutes a breach of protocol and will trigger automated security measures, including on-site quarantine and memory suppression procedures.


Personnel operating indirect observation equipment must wear full protective gear, including sensory insulation apparatus designed to block all external stimuli, to mitigate potential anomalous influence. All operations must be conducted under the direct supervision of at least one Level A personnel and must maintain a minimum distance of 50 meters from the A-L-024 containment zone.

Containment Protocols

A-L-024 is to be contained within five hermetically sealed containers constructed from heat-resistant, high-strength alloy, each measuring 30 cm x 10 cm x 10 cm. Each unit must incorporate an independent multi-lock security mechanism and be equipped with environmental monitoring sensors providing continuous telemetry of temperature, humidity, and anomalous energy fluctuations. All containment units are to be housed within a dedicated containment chamber located in a high-security subterranean facility, reinforced with multiple tiers of physical and electronic access control systems to prevent unauthorized entry.


Proximity within 30 meters of the A-L-024 containment zone is strictly restricted to Level A personnel or above. Level A personnel authorized to enter must be equipped with specialized protective equipment and accompanied by a minimum of two security officers. Upon exiting the containment area, all personnel are subject to mandatory sensory function assessments and psychological evaluations to detect potential anomalous influence. Any deviation from baseline metrics is to be reported immediately and will result in activation of isolation protocols.


The containment chamber must maintain controlled environmental conditions, with temperature stabilized between 10°C and 15°C and humidity maintained below 40% to suppress potential activity of A-L-024. Any deviation from these parameters must be reported without delay and reviewed by the assigned project lead. Full diagnostic inspections of the chamber’s monitoring systems are to be conducted at 48-hour intervals to verify structural and operational integrity.


In the event of any structural compromise or system anomaly affecting A-L-024 containment units or the chamber environment, the facility is to initiate immediate lockdown procedures. All personnel must evacuate the affected area, and emergency response protocols will be enacted under the authority of the project lead. Any physical contact with A-L-024, whether intentional or accidental, constitutes a critical containment breach. All involved personnel are to be isolated and subjected to comprehensive diagnostic evaluation.

Description
Image of A-L-024 Specimen

This image is an indirect recording of one A-L-024 specimen, captured via a Foundation-engineered non-optical sensing device.

Only My Precious Rose (hereafter referred to as A-L-024) comprises five specimens resembling common roses, classified within the genus Rosa of the family Rosaceae. While its physical appearance closely resembles that of a standard rose, A-L-024 exhibits anomalous properties that affect human sensory perception. Upon any form of direct or indirect sensory contact—particularly visual or tactile—subjects begin to experience progressive degradation of all five senses (sight, taste, smell, hearing, touch). Controlled experiments have confirmed that prolonged exposure to A-L-024 leads to a significant loss of sensory perception with respect to all non-A-L-024 stimuli, eventually resulting in near-complete sensory deprivation except for stimuli originating from A-L-024 itself. Affected individuals report that A-L-024’s color, scent, sound, texture, and flavor become extraordinarily vivid—beyond normal human sensory thresholds—while the rest of the environment becomes increasingly dull, blurred, or unrecognizable. For further information, refer to Document [A-L-024a].


A-L-024 demonstrates a highly potent compulsion effect. Subjects within approximately 10 meters of A-L-024—regardless of prior knowledge or awareness of its properties—report an involuntary and overwhelming desire to approach the specimen. This compulsion often manifests as a strong urge to stare at, touch, or otherwise interact with A-L-024, and in some cases, subjects exhibit profound emotional attachment. Individuals may attempt to verbally or physically express admiration, often referring to A-L-024 using language associated with beauty or perfection. The intensity of this compulsion correlates with both exposure time and proximity, and is particularly pronounced during direct visual contact. Notably, this effect appears to bypass conventional cognitive resistance; even personnel with extensive psychological conditioning have demonstrated limited immunity.


Direct observation of A-L-024—whether through unaided visual inspection or real-time visual transmission—triggers immediate onset of its anomalous effects, resulting in rapid sensory degradation. However, observation via indirect means, such as artistic renderings, still photographs, or pre-recorded video, does not activate the anomaly. This suggests that A-L-024’s effects are intrinsically linked to its "real-time presence," and that mediated representations are incapable of conveying its sensory influence. For supporting experimental data and incident documentation, refer to Incident [A-L-024b].


Further observation indicates that A-L-024's anomalous effects are not limited to human subjects. Mammalian test subjects (e.g., laboratory rats and canines) exposed to A-L-024 exhibit similar signs of fixation, accompanied by diminished responsiveness to other sensory stimuli in the environment. The extent to which A-L-024 affects plant life or inorganic matter remains inconclusive due to insufficient data; however, preliminary trials suggest the anomaly predominantly targets organisms possessing advanced sensory systems.

Incident [A-L-024b]

於██/██/20██，兩位C級研究人員於執行A-L-024常規監測任務期間，透過實時影像傳輸系統觀察目標時，突然回報視覺感知異常。受試者聲稱其視野內色彩飽和度顯著衰減，唯獨A-L-024本體維持原有色調。


事件發生後，立即啟動緊急協議：

  • 立即終止所有A-L-024影像數據流傳輸
  • 受影響人員進行隔離觀察
  • 對相關人員實施記憶刪除程序

⚠ 基於此次事件，所有針對A-L-024的直接視覺觀察實驗均被無限期禁止，未來僅允許透過間接方式（如數據分析、非光學感測器等）進行研究。
