站點坐標

站點-G15，德國羅斯托克

The UNC Foundation's primary aim is to ensure the safe containment and protection of anomalous objects, entities, and phenomena, while reducing potential harm to humanity.
The Foundation operates globally, with extensive facilities and branches, and is dedicated to providing highly specialized training to Foundation personnel, known as Foundation operatives, to enable mastery of skills in fields such as science, engineering, and containment procedures.
As Foundation operatives, personnel assume significant personal risk to tirelessly monitor and contain anomalous objects and phenomena.
As a highly confidential organization, the public is completely unaware of the existence of the UNC Foundation.
Due to our winning the trust and support of major government institutions worldwide, we enjoy special powers to assist in containment operations and research around the world.
